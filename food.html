Discover Thai Cuisine

Thai cuisine is the most flavorful and nutritious food in the world.

You will fall in love with Thai food whether simple street food, ordinary restaurant or extraordinary chef-inspired cuisine.








Thailand is considered a paradise not only for its breathtaking beauty and inspiring culture but also for its culinary brilliance. Thai cuisine is as rich and diverse as its culture. Uniquely crafted to appeal to all tastes, Thai food combines the best of flavors, textures, colors, and presentation. Thai cusine is wealth of ancient cooking secrets and Thai cuisine offers enriching and memorable dining experiences.



For Thais, rice is life and forms the center of any meal. It is the soul of Thai cooking, whereas the essence of authentic Thai cuisine lies in its herbs and spices. Not only do these herbs add wonderful tastes and aromas to the food, but they also give a tremendous nutritious. It is common to be served dishes that include spicy, sour, sweet and salty flavors at one meal.

  • Jay O [Michelin Guide]

    Jay Oh might be known for Mama Noodles Tom Yum, but her menu contains numerous top-notch dishes such as fried tofu with crispy garlic, boiled rice with duck, and signature crispy pork.

  • Jay Fai [One Michelin Star]

    Jay Fai has long been a street food icon and for the signature crispy golden-brown crab omelets and stir-fried noodles with seafood and gravy. Most of her famous menu are seafood.

  • Guay Tiew Khua Gai Suanmali [Michelin Guide]

    This long-standing stir-fried chicken noodle shop is a hidden gem. The chicken is well marinated, tasty and soft, while the noodles are crispy on the outside and soft on the inside.

  • Methavalai Sorndaeng [One Michelin Star]

    One of the original Thai fine dining restaurants, using the same timeless recipes. Highlights are crispy rice noodles, spicy lemongrass salad, and crab meat and acacia red curry.

  • Praya Dining [Michelin Guide]

    The menu unites iconic dishes from the Sukhothai and Ayutthaya and modern Bangkok; roasted shrimp dip with crispy catfish salad and stir-fried pork in yellow chili paste are recommended.

  • Plu [Michelin Guide]

    Among the beautifully presented authentic pan-Thailand dishes, The signature are braised pork belly in five-spiced, with one of the restaurant signature cocktails.

  • Ong Tong

    Ongtong is known for Northern cuisine. The signature dish is Khaosoi, a creamy and slightly spicy yellow curry noodle soup and ca be served as a stir-fried noodle.

  • Somtum Der

    Somtum Der is well-known for north-eastern dishes, The homemade grilled sausage and spicy herbal soup are standouts, but the signature is Som Tam, the famous Thai papaya salad.

  • Mahasan

    This is ne of the hardest restaurants to get a table. Mahasan uses meat from Thai-raised Thai-French cows to make mouthwatering dishes such as its signature beef with rice cooked with fat and herbs.
