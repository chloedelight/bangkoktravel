Local Activities And Attractions

  • Thai Traditional Costume

    Rent Thai traditional costume from Sense of Thai and take nice photo at The Grand Palace (Wat Phra Kaew)

  • Cruise on Chaophraya river

    Cruise along the river to discover the city with many choices of boat. You can enjoy the view with dinner

  • Sky Bar at Lebua

    Sky is one of the highest rooftop cocktials in the world. Come for magic hour, or sunset, or after dark.

  • Asiatique

    Bangkok's first large-scale riverside entertainment complex combined shopping, dining, sightseeing, activities and events.

  • Rodfai Market

    Rod Fai Market is a remarkable night bazaar for teenagers and it is located on some abandoned train track.

  • Lumpini Park

    Bangkok's the biggest park and has managed to remain an oasis at the heart of the city. You can rent a boat and paddle around the lake or relax under a tree.

  • Wat Arun

    The famous Temple of the Dawn, is one of the best-known landmarks in the city and located on the west of Chaophraya river. This temple is sparkles in the sunlight.

  • Bangkok Art & Culture Centre

    This is centre for cultural exchange of the international art scene in Bangkok where you can explore spacious galleries, cafés, restaurants and bookshops.

  • Damnoen Saduak Floating Market

    Damnoen Saduak Floating Market is a 19-mile-long canal that framed by busy boats and stalls selling food, snack, fruit and all sorts of souvenirs and clothing.
